This fully-updated second edition remains the only truly detailed exploration of the origins of our Solar System, written by an authority in the field. Spacecraft and landers have explored the planets of the Solar System, observations have been made of Solar-System bodies outside the region of the planets and planets have been detected and observed around many solar-type stars.
