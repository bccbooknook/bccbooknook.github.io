About BCCBookNook

BCCBookNook is a website solely and exclusively developed for the Library of Baao Community College.The site contains the books available from the Library, gallery tab dedicated for school images and informative updates from the school administration and library personnel. With BCCBookNook, the users would be able to access for their books with ease. Maximizing the capability of the Internet to have a quicker inquiry even without their presence to the physical library.
